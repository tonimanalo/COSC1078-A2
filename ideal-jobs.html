Dhiraj and Aiden – Development

Both Raj and Zane’s ideal jobs involve being part of the bigger picture. Unlike the other members of the team that work directly with the software they are developing or implementing, Raj and Zane’s ideal jobs consist of thinking about implementation on a broader scale for clients or companies. Zane’s focus would be more on team efforts in his own company to work on development of software and on client needs of a specific software. Both Raj and Zane have lofty ideals as these roles would not just encompass knowing the ins and outs of a wide array of technologies, coding languages, communication technologies and engineering skills but would also require out of IT skills such as leadership, organization and knowing the direction of their respective companies/clients in order to lead others towards the same goal.

Both Toni and Nadvi need the programing and understanding skills for their respective fields yes, but also need a passion for creative thinking in fields that are high in demand in the IT world.
