Overview

A project idea I would like to innovate is a mobile application consisting of a shared calendar amongst a group of users. T he features included in this calendar are inputs of their events, linking other calendars to the shared calendar and personalize it to their liking. All users input their weekly schedules, then the app will combine everyone’s schedule to find the times when everyone is available. This can be used for anything from social sport gatherings to night outs or even just a trip to the movies. This helps the whole organization period a little easier. In addition to the shared calendar, the application will also feature a section that gathers information of restaurants in the city or the area the user is located in. Another function of the application gives the user the ability to input their chosen songs from Spotify into a shared playlist amongst the group of users. So, whether you’re playing a sport, travelling to a destination, or hitting the dance floor, the music will be on point with everyone’s desired music taste implemented into the playlist rotation.

Motivation

The motivation behind this project idea is to assist groups of friends to organise social events or gatherings more efficiently. Entering adulthood made it hard for us to regularly meet and catch up while juggling with university and work whereas compared to high school, it was easier for us to see each other. Also, when organizing meetups, it’s very difficult to get everyone to pay attention when they need to give a response on a pending hang out without someone posting a meme, sending a TikTok or continuing a joke that was said previously. Another motivation for this application was for a group of users to have a shared playlist of songs. This allows users to discover even more songs from their friends and can also be a very good conversation starter. Therefore, this project idea was to create a mobile application that enables us to share each other’s schedules to help us determine when a group of users are all free to meet up and to also browse top or trending restaurants on the side to decide where we can eat and catch-up, while having a shared song playlist with other users.

Description

The application is similar to your average calendar application on your smartphone like adding events, tasks or reminders, importing and exporting calendars and staying organised. However, the project idea we have come up with have added features that makes it very unique. One of the features that this mobile application has is that the user is able to share their calendar with friends or family and vice versa. This feature allows the user and the group they shared the calendar with to easily find an available time and day to gather and catch up. It makes it much easier and more efficient to block out the time and days they are not available rather than typing it in a group chat or saying it in person and eventually forgetting about it.
Another feature this product has is the ability for the user and their group to personalise the calendar however they like. Different types of groups of people have different aesthetics. We want to ensure this feature is in the mobile application as the user is free to customise their calendar to their liking with different themes and colours to create a sense of personalisation.
The Spotify connection feature will allow each person to link their Spotify account within the app. This allows each person to pick 15 songs each which will then be added to a communal playlist. These 15 songs can be changed at the start of each week to keep everyone happy as new music is released, and new tunes are discovered. Having this set number of songs gives everyone an equal chance to have their tracks played. This removes the chance of someone spamming songs into the playlist and ruining the variety of songs. This feature also removes the inconvenience of picking songs with multiple music tastes in a group. A ‘self-skip’ feature will also be included that only allows the owner of the song or the person who chose the song for the playlist to skip the song.
You will also be able to create different groups within the app. For example, you might have your basketball team group, your main social group or even a family group. This will allow you to tailor what music you want for each group. In the basketball group you would want more upbeat and enthusiastic music. For the social group, you could add chill or workout music. For family, you can add songs that focuses on the classics that parents or elderly people will like and keeping it family friendly.
An additional feature this mobile application has is a service that lists restaurants nearby the users location, trending places to eat, restaurants with specific cuisines and search restaurants. In addition to the services listed above, the user can look at the menu of the restaurant, read the reviews, make a booking and view the opening hours of the restaurant. This feature makes it easier for the user to plan ahead of time and find a place to eat.

To conduct this development, you will need a Mac computer that is updated to the latest version to optimize performance.
  • We can then set up a GitHub repository, to allow our team to work collaboratively on the app. GitHub also allows for features like branching, which allows you to keep the main release of the app running, while adding new features to future releases. A project management tool such as Jira could also be used to keep track of the features that must be added to the app and assign said features to different members of the team.
  • While the app is in development, it is important to make sure that we are testing as we code, to ensure that we don’t miss out on any major bugs when the app is released. We can use a continuous integration system like CircleCI to catch any bugs that may come up as we add new features to the code, automatically.
  • Once we have a build that is viable for release, we must then think about deploying the app. We can use a platform like Docker, in order to containerize our app, which will enable us to host the app in a similar environment to the one it was developed in, which would also limit the number of unforseen bugs that may arise.
  • The last step in our deployment phase would be to consider what cloud platform we would use. For our case, AWS provides an easy to set up hosting platform. We could use their Elastic Beanstalk service in combination with AWS CloudFront to facilitate our content delivery
  • Now that we have a version for release, we need to test our app. We could use AWS Device Farm to set up large-scale testing with multiple devices. It is vital we do this to catch as many bugs as possible early in the products' life cycle
  • We then move on to alpha and beta phases, to further test our apps in real world scenarios. If all goes well, we will be able to set ourselves up for our first public release. Once our app is on the AppStore, we can now start thinking about SEO techniques, to make our app be seen by as many people as possible. We might even consider paid social media marketing to increase our reach even further

Outcome

If the project is successful, we hope that it would help users and their group of friends to easily find a date and time where they are all available to meet up or catch up with each other. Users would also have an application that would add each user’s current song rotation into a shared playlist with friends. We also hope that it would also find it easier for the user and their friends to find a restaurant where they can catch up and enjoy good food. The impact of this app would be efficient to those who have a busy schedule yet want to make time to meet those who are dear to them while eating at good places and to listen to each other’s music taste.
