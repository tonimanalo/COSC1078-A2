Project Ideas


overview

A project idea I would like to innovate is a mobile application consisting of a shared calendar amongst a group of users. The features included in this calendar are inputs of their events, linking other calendars to the shared calendar and personalise it to their liking. All users input their weekly schedules, then the app will combine everyone’s schedule to find the times when everyone is available. This can be used for anything from social sport gatherings to night outs or even just a trip to the movies. This helps the whole organisation period a little easier. In addition to the shared calendar, the application will also feature a section that gathers information of restaurants in the city or the area the user is located in. Another function of the application gives the user the ability to input their chosen songs from Spotify into a shared playlist amongst the group of users. So, whether you’re playing a sport, travelling to a destination, or hitting the dance floor, the music will be on point with everyone’s desired music taste implemented into the playlist rotation.

Motivation

The motivation behind this project idea is to assist groups of friends to organise social events or gatherings more efficiently. Entering adulthood made it hard for us to regularly meet and catch up while juggling with university and work whereas compared to high school, it was easier for us to see each other. Also, when organising meetups, it’s very difficult to get everyone to pay attention when they need to give a response on a pending hang out without someone posting a meme, sending a TikTok or continuing a joke that was said previously. Another motivation for this application was for a group of users to have a shared playlist of songs. This allows users to discover even more songs from their friends and can also be a very good conversation starter. Therefore, this project idea was to create a mobile application that enables us to share each other’s schedules to help us determine when a group of users are all free to meet up and to also browse top or trending restaurants on the side to decide where we can eat and catch-up, while having a shared song playlist with other users.
